Matthew Perry

Here's a timeline of Matthew's life

  • 1969 - Born in Williamstown, Massachusetts
  • 1994 - Begins his role as Chandler Bing in the TV series "Friends," gaining widespread fame
  • 2002 - Stars in the film "The Whole Nine Yards"
  • 2004 - Appears in "The Whole Ten Yards" and stars in the TV series "The West Wing"
  • 2011 - Stars in the TV series "Mr. Sunshine"
  • 2015 - Acts in "The End of Longing" in London's West End
  • 2020 - Joins the cast of the TV series "The Flight Attendant"
  • 2023 - Dies at the age of 54
